How to use MultiRoblox
Quick guide to get started
① Adding Accounts
Click + Add Account in the sidebar. Choose Login Page to sign in via Chrome browser — your cookie is captured automatically. Or choose Paste Cookie to paste a .ROBLOSECURITY cookie directly.
② Launching Accounts
Click the Launch button on any account card to open Roblox with that account. You can launch multiple accounts simultaneously — each runs in its own isolated Roblox instance.
③ Generator
Go to the Generator tab. Paste your BloxGen API key (starts with BLOX-) from bloxgen.net, then click Generate. The combo and cookie are shown and copied to clipboard automatically.
④ Fast Flags
The FFlags tab lets you set Roblox client flags (like FPS unlocking or graphics tweaks). Changes are saved to ClientAppSettings.json and applied on the next Roblox launch.
⑤ Getting Your Cookie
Open Chrome → go to roblox.com → press F12 → Application → Cookies → .ROBLOSECURITY. Copy the value and paste it into MultiRoblox.
